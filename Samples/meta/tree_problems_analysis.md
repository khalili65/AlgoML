---

## Quick Pattern Match

- **Void Return**: Inorder Traversal
- **Single Value**: Max Depth, Min Depth
- **Boolean Return**: Validate BST, Symmetric Tree, Same Tree, Balanced Tree
- **TreeNode Return**: Construct from Preorder+Inorder, Convert Sorted Array/List to BST, Unique BSTs II
- **BFS**: Level Order, Zigzag, Level Order II
